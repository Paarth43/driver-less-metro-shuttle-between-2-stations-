# driver-less-metro-shuttle-between-2-stations-
driver-less metro (shuttle between 2 stations) with the help of 2 ir sensors which detect wall at the end as station and break the metro, servo as gate, buzzer to to alert of arrival and gate opening, 2 more ir sensors to detect passanger and the counting of passengers shown in i2c lcd, one l289n motor driver to control 4 bo gear
